Rawi v3 Arabic diacritization model

This app bundles the quantized `rawi_v3.int8.onnx` model and its vocabulary
from [TigreGotico/Rawi-v3](https://huggingface.co/TigreGotico/Rawi-v3).

- License: Apache License 2.0 (see LICENSE.txt in this directory)
- Model SHA-256: `e1253c5c5edfab3a7d47167323413cb3253d26df79eedb6db02100cbbd22ecb7`
- Vocabulary SHA-256: `5a8972bc1c5985092973d543d8b04f4c9317b2d11625c242420baf69bb4bf037`

The model runs entirely on the device through ONNX Runtime React Native. Audio,
transcripts, and generated vowel marks are not sent to the model author or to a
remote inference API.
