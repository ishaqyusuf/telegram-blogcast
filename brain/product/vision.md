## Purpose
Tracks the intended user value, core product narrative, and major use cases this repository is serving.

## Template

### Current Working Vision
- Deliver an owned, high-quality learning experience around Islamic content, long-form posts, audio, books, and channel-driven media.
- Support reading, listening, annotation, saving, and revisiting educational material across devices.

### Core User Jobs
- Discover and consume rich educational content.
- Listen to audio with strong playback continuity.
- Read long-form text and book content in a focused interface.
- Save, comment on, and organize content for later study.
